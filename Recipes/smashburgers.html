Smashburger - With Cheese!!!

The Smashburger is a take on an old American classic cheeseburger, however the smashburger gets it's name from the way you smash down the beef patty on to a piping hot skillet (we suggest a good cast iron skillet!) making the patty juicy in the middle and crispy on the outsides. Add a bit of your favorite cheese and other burger topping and you got yourself a classic smashburger. Enjoy!

What You'll Need

  • 1/4 lb beef
  • Cheese
  • Hamburger bun
  • Toppings of choice

Steps

  1. Preheat your pan to medium hight heat
  2. place a ball of beef onto the skillet
  3. firmly press down - or "smash" flat with a metal spatula
  4. Flip after 2 minutes
  5. Add cheese
  6. Brown your buns
  7. Place patty on bun
  8. Add toppings
  9. Eat
