Easy Key Lime Pie

Traditional Key Lime Pie made with a light grahm cracker pie crust as well as light whipped cream.

What You'll Need

  • 1 pre made grahm cracker pie crust
  • 2 cans sweetened condensed milk
  • Juice of 6 key limes
  • The Zest of 3 of those limes
  • Whipped topping

Steps

  1. Zest 3 limes
  2. Juice 6 limes into a mixing bowl
  3. Add in 2 cans sweetened condensed milk and lime zest
  4. Thorughly mix together
  5. Pour into pie crust
  6. Bake on 400 for 35 minutes
  7. Let rest 20 minutes
  8. Top with whipped topping
  9. Eat Up
