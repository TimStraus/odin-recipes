Beef Kebabs

Traditional Middle Eastern Kebabs with cubed beef and vegetables cooked to perfection on the grill.

What You'll Need

  • 1 lb beef cubed
  • 2 onion
  • 3 bell peppers
  • 1/2 lb of mushrooms
  • Metal or wooden skewers
  • Grill

Steps

  1. Cut beef into 1-2 inch cubes
  2. Cut onion and peppers into roughly 1 inch pieces
  3. Clean mushrooms thoroughly
  4. Alternate placing beef and veggies onto your skewers
  5. Grill on a direct flame, rotate a quarter turn every 2-3 minutes
  6. Take off heat once all sides have browned
  7. Let rest 20 minutes
  8. Eat
